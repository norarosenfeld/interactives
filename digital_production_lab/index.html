Grammy Awards 2025 Mark Historic Moment for Women in Rap

Doechii becomes third woman to win Best Rap Album

By Nora Rosenfeld

With her latest album, Alligator Bites Never Heal, self-proclaimed "Swamp Princess" Doechii makes history as the third woman, and second female artist, to win the award for Best Rap Album. The award was presented to her by Cardi B, who was the first solo female artist to win the award in 2019.

The Tampa-born artist dedicated the win to Black women. She expressed gratitude in her acceptance speech, bringing her mom up on stage with her, and shouted out hometown talent. "There's so much culture in Tampa, whenever people think about Florida they think about, like, Miami, but Tampa has so much talent. Labels! Go to to Tampa, there's talent there, okay?"
